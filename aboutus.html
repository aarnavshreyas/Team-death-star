OUR MISSION

Helping Kids Use Mobile Devices Wisely with AI-Based Solutions

Today, children spend a significant amount of time on cell phones, which can impact their academic performance and social growth. Social media applications, such as games, videos, and messaging apps, may initially seem entertaining but often become distractions, making studies more challenging. We believe that balanced face-to-face interaction is essential for developing key skills like communication, empathy, and teamwork.

Our solution is an AI-powered time management application designed to promote better mobile usage among children. By leveraging machine learning, our application analyzes screen time, suggests study schedules, break times, and offline activities. It predicts optimal screen time based on each child's age, study habits, and emotional health, encouraging a healthy balance between digital engagement and personal growth.

Key Features

Smart Time Management

Our app helps kids allocate time effectively for studies, leisure, and offline activities.

Screen Time Analysis

Analyze screen usage patterns and make intelligent recommendations to encourage productive habits.

Encourages Social Interaction

Promotes offline interaction, enabling children to build essential social and teamwork skills.
